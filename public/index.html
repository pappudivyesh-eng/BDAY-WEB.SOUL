she stepped away

She deactivated Instagram
to focus on life.

And just like that, the conversations stopped. No dramatic ending. No goodbye. Just — quiet.

The sudden silence had a weight to it. Not painful exactly. More like the feeling of a familiar song ending and the room becoming still.

It made me realize something I hadn't put into words before — how much her presence had become part of my routine.

Some absences are loud. Hers was quiet. That's what made it stay.

A Letter
To Shruti
Shruti,

I'm not sure you know how much those conversations meant. The small ones. The ones about nothing in particular. They added up to something I didn't have a word for until later.

You talked about football and studies and the things weighing on you, and I listened — not because I had to, but because I genuinely wanted to know how things were going for you. That felt new.

You made ordinary days feel like they had something worth looking forward to. That's not a small thing. Most people go their whole lives without doing that for someone.

I hope wherever you are right now, things are going the way you want them to. I hope the football is good. I hope the hard things are getting easier.

And I hope you know that somewhere, someone remembers those conversations and is glad they happened.

— with genuine warmth ♡
